Our Journey to
Maritime Excellence

Founded in 2005, ShipDecKK has grown into a premier ship dealership, specializing in high-end yachts, cargo ships, and luxury liners. With a passion for maritime leadership and a commitment to quality, we have been a trailblazing presence that has catered to meet the diverse needs of our global clientele.


From luxury yachts meticulously crafted for discerning captains to vast container ships built to deliver exceptional performance, ShipDecKK stands at the forefront of reliability and outstanding service.

Our Mission

"To provide world-class ships and unparalleled customer service, ensuring every vessel we deliver meets the highest standards of quality, performance, and innovation."

We believe that every ship tells a story, and we are dedicated to helping our clients navigate their journey with confidence and excellence.
